This document recognizes the individuals who have contributed to the design, development, and maintenance of the AevumDB hybrid kernel.

* **Ananda Firmansyah**

## Community Contributors

AevumDB is currently developed and maintained solely by its founder. We are open to contributions and looking for our first community members!

* *Ananda Firmansyah* - Be the first to contribute to the AevumDB kernel.

## How to Get Listed

If you wish to contribute to AevumDB (code, documentation, or bug reports), please follow these steps:

1. Read the guidelines in [CONTRIBUTING.md](CONTRIBUTING.md).
2. Submit a Pull Request with your changes.
3. Once your contribution is merged, you are encouraged to add your name to this list.

## Recognition Criteria

We value high-quality contributions in the following areas:
* Hybrid Kernel Engineering (C++17 & Rust)
* Query Engine Optimization
* Security Research & Memory Safety Audits
* Technical Documentation
* Benchmarking & Performance Profiling

AevumDB is built on the belief that memory safety and high performance should go hand-in-hand.
